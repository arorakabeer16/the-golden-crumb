Our Story

About
The Golden Crumb

Founded by Kabeer Arora, The Golden Crumb began as a simple idea: to bring back the joy of freshly baked, small-batch cookies that feel personal and unforgettable. What started as a passion for experimenting with flavors in a home kitchen quickly turned into a mission to craft cookies that balance comfort and indulgence.

Every batch is made with attention to detail, quality ingredients, and a love for creating moments worth savoring. Today, The Golden Crumb stands for warmth, authenticity, and flavors that feel like home.
